About me

Hi! My name is Lily and I am a sophomore at Syracuse University majoring in International Relations at the Maxwell School of Citizenship and Public Affairs and Magazine, News, and Digital Journalism at the S.I. Newhouse School of Public Communications. I am also a member of the Women's Rowing Team.

I was born and raised in San Fransico. I grew up playing competitive soccer, but made the change to rowing when I walked onto the Syracuse team my freshman year. Back home, I am also a soccer coach for the same club I played for, the San Francisco Seals. I attended the International School of San Francisco and gradauted high school with an International Baccalaureate Diploma. In high school, I wrote for my school's literary magazine and for my school's theatre program.
